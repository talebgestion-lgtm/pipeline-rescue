Pilot terms summary

Clear boundaries before a commercial pilot starts.

These terms summarize the intended operating model for the first Pipeline Rescue pilots. They are a business template, not legal advice, and must be reviewed before signature.

Product scope

  • One controlled HubSpot pilot
  • 30-day measurement window
  • Stalled deal analysis
  • Task and note write-back
  • Weekly review and basic support

Customer duties

  • Provide suitable HubSpot access
  • Confirm pipeline scope
  • Review all recommendations
  • Avoid sensitive data in operator notes
  • Give feedback during the pilot

Human review

Pipeline Rescue does not auto-send customer communications.

All recommendations, tasks, notes, and drafts remain subject to customer review. The pilot is an assistive workflow, not an autonomous sales agent.

Fees

  • Setup: EUR500
  • 30-day pilot: EUR299
  • Continuation: EUR299/month

Limits

  • No guaranteed revenue outcome
  • No production SLA during pilot
  • No CRM migration project
  • No legal compliance guarantee

Stop conditions

  • CRM data is not usable
  • Access cannot be maintained
  • Compliance scope is unresolved
  • Recommendations are not credible

Signature gate

Do not start a paid pilot without identity, payment, privacy, and access confirmed.

Required before launch: provider identity, customer identity, contact email, invoicing method, HubSpot access route, data retention rule, and legal review.
