Pilot privacy notice

Privacy-first scope for a controlled HubSpot pilot.

This page summarizes the intended privacy model for Pipeline Rescue pilots. It is a working notice, not legal advice, and must be reviewed before paid customer use.

Purpose

  • Analyze stalled HubSpot deals
  • Explain risk factors
  • Recommend a next action
  • Create reviewed tasks or notes
  • Measure pilot adoption

Data used

  • Deal identifiers and names
  • Deal stage, amount, close date
  • Owner and activity timestamps
  • Company or contact business context
  • Tasks, notes, and user feedback

Strict limits

Data that must not be intentionally processed

Pipeline Rescue pilots are not designed for health data, legal case data, payment card data, special-category personal data, sensitive employee data, or private notes unrelated to sales activity.

Roles

  • The customer is usually controller for HubSpot CRM data
  • The provider may act as processor for pilot operation
  • Roles must be confirmed before launch

Processors

  • HubSpot as CRM platform
  • OpenAI only if live draft generation is enabled
  • Hosting provider only if externally deployed

Controls

  • Shared-secret access gate
  • No auto-send behavior
  • Human review for drafts
  • Support bundle diagnostics
  • Runtime snapshots and rollback

Before paid use

Confirm legal basis, retention, processors, and support contact.

The public pilot page should not claim full compliance until the actual deployment, processors, data retention, and customer instructions are documented.
