# Initial ICP

## Objective

Pick one initial customer profile for fast pilots and avoid building for every HubSpot user.

## Primary ICP

### Company profile

- B2B SaaS company
- `5` to `30` quota-carrying reps
- HubSpot is the primary CRM for pipeline management
- sales cycle between `30` and `120` days
- average deal value between `3000 EUR` and `50000 EUR`
- at least `50` open deals at a time

### Team profile

- one sales leader who owns pipeline discipline
- at least one ops-capable admin or RevOps contact
- reps already log activities with reasonable consistency

### Pain profile

They already know they lose deals because:

- next steps are not consistently tracked
- deals sit in stage too long
- managers see pipeline issues too late
- reps do not know what to rescue first

## Buyer and user split

### Economic buyer

- VP Sales
- Head of Sales
- Revenue leader in small or mid-size SaaS

### Day-to-day admin buyer

- RevOps lead
- Sales operations manager
- CRM admin in a smaller team

### Daily users

- account executives
- sales managers

## Pilot admission criteria

A pilot account is a good fit only if all of the following are true:

- HubSpot deal pipelines are actively used
- at least `50` open deals exist
- owners are assigned on deals
- reps log tasks, meetings, or notes
- the customer agrees to compare before/after outcomes

## Disqualifiers

Do not prioritize these accounts for the first pilots:

- teams below `3` reps
- companies using HubSpot mainly for marketing, not sales execution
- businesses with extremely short transactional cycles
- teams with almost no CRM hygiene
- teams demanding fully autonomous outbound on day one
- heavily regulated use cases where external drafting is blocked

## Who this is not for in v1

- SDR-heavy outbound platforms
- high-volume transactional ecommerce
- enterprise field sales with deeply custom CRMs
- medical, legal, or public-sector use cases

## Why this ICP wins first

- enough deal volume to show value fast
- enough process to feed the score
- not too complex to integrate
- pain is visible and measurable

## Pilot messaging

Use this message:

> Pipeline Rescue helps HubSpot sales teams catch stalled deals early, explain why they are at risk, and turn that diagnosis into a concrete next action.

Do not use this message:

> Pipeline Rescue is your AI SDR or autonomous sales agent.

## Expansion path after initial ICP

After the first pilots succeed:

1. agencies with multi-client HubSpot practices
2. B2B services firms with consultant-led sales
3. later, larger sales orgs and Salesforce port
