# Publish Checklist

## Objective

Prepare Pipeline Rescue for first public-facing commercial use without overstating maturity.

## Required before outreach

- commercialization kit reviewed
- one-page offer finalized
- demo deck prepared
- demo script rehearsed
- prospecting email customized
- pilot agreement reviewed
- private app onboarding path tested
- support bundle export tested
- clear list of non-promises accepted

## Required before taking payment

- legal identity decided
- invoicing method decided
- payment collection method decided
- pilot agreement reviewed
- basic privacy notice prepared
- customer data handling process documented
- support email ready

## Required before public landing page

- contact email added
- clear pilot offer added
- no guarantee claims
- no autonomous sending claim
- no fake customer logos
- no unverified revenue claim
- pricing and scope match the pilot agreement

## Required before HubSpot marketplace

- OAuth install flow validated on a real portal
- redirect URL deployable on public HTTPS
- support URL available
- documentation URL available
- privacy policy URL available
- terms URL available
- production hosting stable
- review of HubSpot app requirements completed

## First commercial target list

Build a list of `30` prospects:

- `15` B2B SaaS sales leaders
- `10` RevOps / CRM admins
- `5` founder-led B2B sales teams

Only contact prospects that visibly use HubSpot or discuss HubSpot in their stack.

## First week sales activity

- send `10` targeted emails
- book `2` discovery calls
- run `1` product demo
- ask for `1` pilot commitment
- document all objections

## Go-live rule

Do not claim broad SaaS maturity.

Use this wording:

Pipeline Rescue is available as a controlled paid pilot for HubSpot sales teams that want to test stalled-deal recovery on real pipeline data.
