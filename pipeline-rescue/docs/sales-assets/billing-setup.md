# Billing And Payment Setup

## Objective

Define the minimum operational setup needed before accepting paid pilot money.

## Minimum decision before charging

Choose one identity for invoices:

- personal name / sole trader identity
- registered business name, if available later

Do not invent a company name. If operating as an individual, use the real individual identity.

## Required information

Before sending an invoice, prepare:

- legal name used for payment
- contact email
- country
- payment method
- refund rule
- invoice numbering convention
- tax/VAT treatment to confirm
- bank or payment provider details

## Recommended first payment setup

For the first pilots, use the simplest reliable flow:

- invoice manually
- accept bank transfer or payment link
- keep a copy of the signed pilot agreement
- mark payment status manually

Avoid building automated billing until pilots prove demand.

## Suggested pricing

First standard pilot:

- setup: `EUR500`
- 30-day pilot: `EUR299`
- continuation: `EUR299/month`

First reference customer option:

- setup: `EUR0` to `EUR250`
- pilot: `EUR99` to `EUR199`
- condition: written feedback and permission to use anonymized results

## Invoice wording

Line item:

`Pipeline Rescue - 30-day controlled HubSpot stalled-deal recovery pilot`

Description:

`Configuration, controlled access, HubSpot connection support, weekly pilot review, and human-reviewed rescue workflow for stalled HubSpot deals.`

## Payment rule

Recommended:

- setup starts after payment or signed pilot agreement
- pilot starts after HubSpot connection succeeds
- no guaranteed recovered revenue
- cancellation possible at pilot end

## Records to keep

Keep:

- signed pilot agreement
- invoice
- payment confirmation
- customer onboarding email
- privacy notice version sent
- pilot start date
- pilot end date
- support bundle if needed

## Do not do yet

- build subscription billing automation
- promise annual contracts
- promise marketplace distribution
- create complicated pricing tiers for the first customer

## Payment readiness checklist

Before taking money:

- pilot agreement reviewed
- privacy notice reviewed
- invoice identity chosen
- payment method chosen
- support email ready
- customer scope confirmed
- HubSpot access route confirmed
