# Pipeline Rescue One-Page Offer

## Rescue stalled HubSpot deals before they die

Pipeline Rescue helps HubSpot sales teams identify stalled deals, understand why they are at risk, and turn that diagnosis into the next concrete action.

It is not an autonomous AI SDR. It is a focused recovery workflow for deals already in the pipeline.

## Who it is for

- B2B SaaS or B2B service teams
- HubSpot is the active sales CRM
- `5` to `30` sales users
- at least `50` open deals
- sales cycle of `30` to `120` days
- managers already review pipeline health

## What the pilot includes

- HubSpot connection through Private App token or OAuth
- at-risk deal queue
- rescue score and reason explanation
- recommended next action
- HubSpot task creation
- follow-up draft generation with human review
- HubSpot note write-back
- weekly pilot review
- support bundle for diagnostics

## Pilot offer

- setup: `EUR500`
- first 30-day pilot: `EUR299`
- continuation after pilot: `EUR299/month`

First reference customer discount is allowed only in exchange for written feedback and permission to use anonymized results.

## Success criteria

The pilot is considered useful when:

- the customer loads real HubSpot deals successfully
- risk reasons are considered credible
- users create tasks from recommendations
- some at-risk deals receive renewed activity
- feedback is more useful than dismissed

## What is not included

- autonomous sending
- guaranteed recovered revenue
- CRM cleanup consulting
- custom multi-tenant SaaS deployment
- production SLA
- legal compliance guarantee

## Demo path

1. Load live HubSpot queue.
2. Open one at-risk deal.
3. Explain the top risk reasons.
4. Create a HubSpot task.
5. Generate a follow-up draft.
6. Save the draft as a HubSpot note.
7. Review manager report and feedback.

## Positioning line

Pipeline Rescue is the human-reviewed rescue workflow for HubSpot deals that are quietly going cold.
