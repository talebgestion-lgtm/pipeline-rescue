# Outreach Target List

## Objective

Build the first list of prospects for a controlled paid pilot.

Do not buy broad generic data yet. Start with a small, high-fit list and learn from replies.

## Target count

First batch: `30` accounts.

Breakdown:

- `15` B2B SaaS companies using HubSpot
- `10` RevOps / CRM admin profiles
- `5` founder-led B2B sales teams

## Account fit rules

Prioritize accounts when:

- they mention HubSpot publicly
- they sell B2B
- they have a visible sales team
- they likely manage open pipeline
- they post about sales process, RevOps, CRM, pipeline, or forecasting

Deprioritize accounts when:

- they are ecommerce-heavy
- they are only marketing users of HubSpot
- they are very large enterprise with complex procurement
- they operate in sensitive regulated verticals
- they need a fully autonomous sales agent

## Contact roles

Best first contacts:

- Head of Sales
- VP Sales
- Founder / CEO in founder-led sales
- Revenue Operations Manager
- Sales Operations Manager
- HubSpot CRM Admin

## Data fields to collect

For each prospect, collect:

- company name
- website
- LinkedIn URL
- country
- likely HubSpot usage evidence
- sales team size estimate
- buyer name
- buyer role
- buyer LinkedIn URL
- email if available
- first message date
- reply status
- next action
- notes

## Outreach sequence

Use:

1. first email
2. follow-up after `3` business days
3. final follow-up after `7` business days

Stop after the final follow-up unless they engage.

## Qualification call goal

The goal is not to sell everything.

The goal is to answer:

- do they have stale deal pain?
- do they use HubSpot enough?
- can they give access for a controlled pilot?
- is there a buyer who cares?
- is the workflow acceptable without auto-send?

## Daily target

Minimum first push:

- identify `10` accounts
- send `5` messages
- log every response
- book `1` demo attempt

## Do not do

- scrape sensitive personal data
- spam generic lists
- promise revenue recovery
- claim HubSpot marketplace certification
- claim autonomous AI sending
