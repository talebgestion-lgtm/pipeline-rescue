# Market Audit

## Thesis

The opportunity is not "build another AI sales copilot."

The opportunity is to solve a narrower, painful, measurable problem:

**stalled deals that quietly die in HubSpot pipelines**

## Why this wedge

- The problem is frequent.
- The ROI is easy to explain.
- The workflow is already structured in HubSpot.
- The output can be embedded directly in the CRM.

## What to avoid

- generic AI SDR positioning
- broad prospecting assistant claims
- fully autonomous outbound from day one
- dashboards that live outside HubSpot

## Competitive pressure

Pipeline Rescue would compete indirectly with:

- HubSpot native AI writing and prospecting tools
- AI SDR products on the HubSpot Marketplace
- workflow automation products that already create follow-up tasks

This means the product must win on:

- narrower scope
- stronger deal-risk explanation
- better prioritization
- lower cognitive load

## Winning message

Do not sell "AI follow-up."

Sell:

**Rescue stalled deals before they die.**

## Distribution reality

HubSpot Marketplace is important, but not the real starting point.

The product should first be sold directly to a few pilot customers, then listed, then optimized for certification.

## MVP fit with HubSpot

Best surfaces:

- deal record app card
- pipeline summary page
- task creation
- custom workflow action

## Final recommendation

- Platform: HubSpot first
- Wedge: stalled deal rescue
- Commercial motion: pilot sales first, marketplace second
